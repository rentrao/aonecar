About A One Ride

Learn more about our journey, mission, and the team behind your premium rental experience

Our Story

Founded in 2020, A One Ride emerged with a simple vision: to revolutionize the self-drive rental industry in Indore by offering premium vehicles with exceptional customer service.

What started as a small fleet of 5 cars has now grown to include over 50 vehicles, including luxury sedans, SUVs, and premium bikes, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the city.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for self-drive rentals in Indore.
